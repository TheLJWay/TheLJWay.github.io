Labyrinth

Labyrinth is a rogue-like FPS made with Unity. My contributions to this game was planning and sketching the initial concept. As the team's Art Director, designing the levels (rooms), objects, and sounds that would be used was one of my priorities. The game would randomly generate a "labyrinth" where the player must traverse with the goal of encountering the final whale boss whom they must defeat to win the game. Each playthrough would always be unique considering the rogue-like genre.
